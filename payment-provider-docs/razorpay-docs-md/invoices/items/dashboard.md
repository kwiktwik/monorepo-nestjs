Items are products or services that you can add to [Invoices](/razorpay-docs-md/invoices.md) and charge to customers. You can create an item on your Dashboard from **Invoice** → **Items**. Once created, it appears on the list of items in the Dashboard.

## Create an Item

To create an item via Dashboard:

1. Log in to the Dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Invoices** → **Items**.
3. Click **New Item**.
4. Enter the following:
   1. **Name**
   2. **Rate**
   3. **Description**

![Create an item in dashboard fill details](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/invoices/items/build/browser/assets/images/items-add-item.jpg)

Once an item is created, it will appear on the list of created item and also in the drop-down menu at the time of invoice creation.

![List of created items](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/invoices/items/build/browser/assets/images/items-item-list.jpg)

## Edit an Item

To update the details of an item:

1. Navigate to **Invoices** → **Items**.
2. In the item list, click on the desired **Item Id**.

   ![](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/invoices/items/build/browser/assets/images/items-item-list.jpg)
3. In the **Edit Item** page, enter the following:
   1. **Name**
   2. **Rate**
   3. **Description**
4. Click **Save**.

## Delete an Item

To delete an item:

1. Navigate to **Invoices** → **Items**.
2. Hover over the desired item and click **delete**.

![Delete items option](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/invoices/items/build/browser/assets/images/items-delete-item.jpg)

**Watch Out!**

You cannot delete an item with which invoices have already been created.
