Razorpay Payments Mobile App helps you to track customer payments even when you are on-the-move. This app enables you to:

- View real-time status on payments made by customers.
- Accept payments from your customers using Razorpay.me URL and Payment Links.
- Issue refunds to customers.
- View and track settlement details.
- Generate API keys.

## Advantages

- Payment and settlement data now at your fingertips.
- Razorpay.me URL created instantly after activation.
- Failed payment? No problem! Create and share a Payment Link.
- Issue refunds in a few taps.
