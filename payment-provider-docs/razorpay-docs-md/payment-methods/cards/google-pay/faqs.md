1. #### [Can customers pay using cards on Google Pay on iOS and web apps?](/razorpay-docs-md/payment-methods/cards/google-pay/faqs.md#can-customers-pay-using-cards-on-google-pay)

   No, card payments via Google Pay is supported only on Android apps.
2. #### [What are the supported card networks and issuers?](/razorpay-docs-md/payment-methods/cards/google-pay/faqs.md#what-are-the-supported-card-networks-and-issuers)

   Google Pay allows Visa cards issued by the following banks:

   - Axis Bank.
   - Kotak Mahindra Bank.
   - State Bank of India.
   - HDFC Bank.
   - Standard Chartered Bank.
3. #### [I have already integrated with Razorpay Google Pay SDK. Do I need to re-integrate?](/razorpay-docs-md/payment-methods/cards/google-pay/faqs.md#i-have-already-integrated-with-razorpay-google-pay)

   No, you do not have to re-integrate. This feature works with existing integrations of Razorpay Google Pay SDK.
4. #### [Will the `Pay via Cards on Google Pay` option appear if the customer has not added a card?](/razorpay-docs-md/payment-methods/cards/google-pay/faqs.md#will-the-pay-via-cards-on-google-pay)

   No, the card must be saved on Google Pay before initiating the payment. Customers are not given the option to save card details while making a payment.
5. #### [Can the customer add a card while making a payment on my app?](/razorpay-docs-md/payment-methods/cards/google-pay/faqs.md#can-the-customer-add-a-card-while-making)

   No, the customer cannot add a card while making a payment. They must add the card on Google Pay before initiating the transaction on your app.
6. #### [Does the customer need to load any money to make payment via card?](/razorpay-docs-md/payment-methods/cards/google-pay/faqs.md#does-the-customer-need-to-load-any-money)

   No, as this is not a wallet, the customer does not have to load any money to make payments.
