Using Razorpay you can accept payments from your customers using Corporate Netbanking. The customers enter their Corporate Netbanking credentials to make payments.

## Enable Corporate Netbanking

You need to get corporate netbanking enabled for your Razorpay account.

To do this:

1. Log in to the Dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Settings** → **Payment Methods**.
3. Click **Netbanking** → **Corporate Netbanking**. This shows the list of banks already active for your account.

   ![Already Enabled Banks](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/payment-methods/netbanking/build/browser/assets/images/payment-methods-netbanking-existing-banks.jpg)

   You can activate more banks. To do this:
   1. Click **Add more Banks**
   2. Click **Request** against the bank you want to add.

      ![Add a Bank](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/payment-methods/netbanking/build/browser/assets/images/payment-methods-netbanking-add-bank.jpg)
   3. Click **Confirm**.

Our support team will enable the bank for your account.

Know how to [enable payment methods from the Dashboard](/razorpay-docs-md/dashboard/account-settings/payment-methods.md).
