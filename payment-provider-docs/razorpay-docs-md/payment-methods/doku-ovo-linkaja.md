DOKU, OVO, and LinkAja are prominent digital payment platforms in Indonesia, enabling consumers to make purchases using their Wallet. By integrating DOKU, OVO or LinkAja, you can offer a secure and seamless payment experience tailored to the Indonesian market.

Advantages

- **Digital Wallet Integration**: All three platforms integrate digital wallet functionalities, allowing users to make secure cashless payments.
- **Available in Indonesia**: DOKU, OVO and LinkAja are widely used in Indonesia and support transactions in Indonesian Rupiah (IDR).
- **Session Timeout**: Each platform enforces a session timeout of 1 hour for completing transactions.
- **PPRO Integration**: DOKU, OVO and LinkAja are integrated via the PPRO payment processing system, providing customers with a streamlined payment experience.

### Refunds and Settlements

Payments follow standard refund and settlement timelines.

- You can refund customer payments made using DOKU, OVO, and LinkAja by following the usual [refunds](/razorpay-docs-md/refunds.md)

  process.
- DOKU, OVO, and LinkAja payments take T+22 days to get settled to your Razorpay account.
