You can use Payment Pages to build webpages with customised content, ready-to-use templates, rich-text support, media support and social media sharing options. Share the Payment Page link with your customers and start accepting payments instantly.

Create Razorpay Payment Pages with ready-to-use templates and collect payments.

## Create Payment Page

Follow these steps to create a Payment Page:

1. Log in with your Dashboard credentials.

   ![login](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/mobile-app/accept-payments/build/browser/assets/images/payments-mobile-app-login.jpg)
2. Tap **Payment Pages** under the **Accept Payments** section.

   ![Accept Payments Section](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/mobile-app/accept-payments/build/browser/assets/images/pp-mobile-app-main.jpg)
3. Tap **+ Create Payment Page** and select the template of your choice.

   ![login](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/mobile-app/accept-payments/build/browser/assets/images/pl-template-mobile-app.gif)
4. Add business details such as page title, description, contact information and so on. Know more about how to add [business details](/razorpay-docs-md/payment-pages/create.md#add-business-details)   .
5. You can also add a goal tracker to track your goals. To add a goal tracker, tap **+ Add a Goal Tracker**. Know more about how to [configure a goal tracker](/razorpay-docs-md/payment-pages/goal-tracker.md)   .

   ![Payment Page Goal Tracker](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/mobile-app/accept-payments/build/browser/assets/images/pp-mobile-goal-tracker.jpg)
6. Add payment details such as price field, email, phone number and so on. Know more about how to add [payment details](/razorpay-docs-md/payment-pages/create.md#add-payment-details)   . Tap **Publish Page**.

   ![Payment Page Publish Page](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/mobile-app/accept-payments/build/browser/assets/images/pp-mobile-publish-page.jpg)

## Share Payment Pages

You can share the Payment Pages using:

- SMS and Email
- Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp and more.

## Edit Payment Page

Follow these steps to edit a Payment Page:

1. Under the **Accept Payments** section, tap **Payment Pages** and scroll down to the list of Payment Pages.

   ![Payment Pages List](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/mobile-app/accept-payments/build/browser/assets/images/pp-mobile-list.jpg)
2. Tap on the payment page you wish to edit.
3. Tap **Edit Page**.

   ![Payment Pages Edit](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/mobile-app/accept-payments/build/browser/assets/images/pp-mobile-edit-page.jpg)
4. The page appears in edit mode. You can now edit any of the fields to update the details, including the price fields.

## Deactivate Payment Page

Follow these steps to deactivate a Payment Page:

1. Under the **Accept Payments** section, tap **Payment Pages** and scroll down to the list of Payment Pages.

   ![Payment Pages List](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/mobile-app/accept-payments/build/browser/assets/images/pp-mobile-list.jpg)
2. Tap on the payment page you wish to deactivate.
3. Tap **Deactivate**.

   ![click cancel payment link](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/mobile-app/accept-payments/build/browser/assets/images/pp-mobile-deactivate.jpg)
4. Tap **Yes, deactivate** to confirm.
