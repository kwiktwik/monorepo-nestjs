Using Dashboard you can create and manage virtual bank accounts and UPI IDs tagged to specific customers.

To access Smart Collect:

1. Log in to Dashboard.
2. Click **Smart Collect** from the left pane.

You can perform the following actions:

- [Create Customer Identifiers](/razorpay-docs-md/smart-collect/va-vpa-qr/dashboard/create.md)
- [Make Test Payments](/razorpay-docs-md/smart-collect/va-vpa-qr/dashboard/test-payments.md)
- [Refund Payments](/razorpay-docs-md/smart-collect/va-vpa-qr/dashboard/refund.md)
- [Close Customer Identifiers](/razorpay-docs-md/smart-collect/va-vpa-qr/dashboard/close.md)
- [Search Customer Identifiers](/razorpay-docs-md/smart-collect/va-vpa-qr/dashboard/search.md)
