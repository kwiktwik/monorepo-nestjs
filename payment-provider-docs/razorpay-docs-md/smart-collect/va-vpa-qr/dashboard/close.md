Customer Identifiers can be closed in two ways:

- Automatically, by specifying a **Close By** date and time during [Customer Identifier creation](/razorpay-docs-md/smart-collect/va-vpa-qr/dashboard/create.md)  .
- Manually, using the **Close Account** button on the Customer Identifier details pane:
  1. In the list, select the Customer Identifier ID.
  2. In the right pane that appears, click **Close Account**.

  ![](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/smart-collect/va-vpa-qr/dashboard/build/browser/assets/images/smart-collect-va_close.jpg)

  1. A dialog box appears to confirm the closing. Click **Yes**.
