# Collect Request Solution

---

PhonePe Charge APIs are used primarily for offline organized merchants. PhonePe APIs are integrated with the merchant Billing POS. A merchant can request a payment from a customer by sending a collect request to the user’s PhonePe app.

A merchant does Offline Integration primarily for:

- **Accepting Payment at Offline Stores**: The merchant sends collect request at the time of checkout.
- **Offline Delivery**: The merchant sends the collect request at the time of checkout. The customer receives a notification on their PhonePe app and completes the payment once the good/service is delivered.

## POS Based Solutions

These solutions can be merged with your point-of-sale systems for easy record-keeping and management. Such a set-up is ideal for stores with high-volume sales, be it local shops or bigger stores.

## Collect Request Solution

This option allows you to auto populate the amount your customers need to pay, rather than manually entering it everytime. Your customers get a collect request on their phones and all they need to do is use their PhonePe app to complete the payment.
