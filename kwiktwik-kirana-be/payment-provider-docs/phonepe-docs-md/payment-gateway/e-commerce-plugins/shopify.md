# Shopify Integration

---

Shopify provides a simple, user friendly platform for managing your online store. You can easily integrate the PhonePe Payment Gateway, even without a technical background, by simply installing our official plugin. This straightforward, no code process allows you to start accepting payments and managing orders effortlessly.

## Integrating PhonePe Payment Gateway with Shopify

⚠️ ****Admins Only**!**

---

Only users with Admin access can set up and integrate PhonePe Payment Gateway.

## 1. Installation and Account Linking

- Navigate to your payment settings by going to Settings → Payments. (You may also find a “Payments” card on your homepage). Click Edit.

[](/static/354e782092d95791514c2fd6f024f373/Shopify-1.mp4)

[](/static/354e782092d95791514c2fd6f024f373/Shopify-1.mp4)

- Now click +Add Payment

[](/static/c06d76f76aba78f19a895c2a9da45c5e/Shopify-1-1.mp4)

[](/static/c06d76f76aba78f19a895c2a9da45c5e/Shopify-1-1.mp4)

- Select the Search by provider tab. Search for “PhonePe PG” and select it from the results.

[](/static/f1e7979e0ece0b5e35abdacf353fd4c0/Shopify-3.mp4)

[](/static/f1e7979e0ece0b5e35abdacf353fd4c0/Shopify-3.mp4)

- Click the Install button to install the plugin.

[](/static/2c7ca790f516bc7d7b514d124b0d5b18/Shopify-4.mp4)

- Next, you must link your PhonePe MID (Merchant ID) to your Shopify store. You will be redirected to a login page. Enter the email ID and Password provided by the PhonePe team during your onboarding to log in to your PhonePe Business dashboard. Once successfully linked, you will be automatically redirected back to your store.

## 2. Testing Your Integration (UAT Test Mode)

Before accepting live payments, you must test the payment flow. To do this, you will need to enable Test Mode.

- To enable Test Mode, go to Settings → Payments and click on PhonePe. Switch the Enable Test Mode toggle ON and click Activate. Your test mode will be activated.

[](/static/c1435be65bba274947f7b4f23cc5ea46/Shopify-6.mp4)

[](/static/c1435be65bba274947f7b4f23cc5ea46/Shopify-6.mp4)

- To check the payment flow, go to your public store website (as a customer).

- Select any item and add it to your cart.

[](/static/f0ccb3b81e96e6b39d1e558fbdf133af/Shopify-7.mp4)

[](/static/f0ccb3b81e96e6b39d1e558fbdf133af/Shopify-7.mp4)

- Proceed to checkout. Enter your customer details and make sure you see PhonePe as a payment methodn and click Pay now.

[](/static/131f240c3cbf7581a9e9869ada816f11/Shopify-8.mp4)

[](/static/131f240c3cbf7581a9e9869ada816f11/Shopify-8.mp4)

- Since you are using UAT (Test Mode), you will see the PhonePe checkout page simulator.

[](/static/70756c187afa9cffcfc4e6b7a9de4a97/Shopify-9.mp4)

[](/static/70756c187afa9cffcfc4e6b7a9de4a97/Shopify-9.mp4)

## 3. Going Live (Production Mode)

To set your environment for production, return to the PhonePe settings (Settings → Payments → PhonePe) and disable the Test Mode toggle. Your gateway is now live, and real transactions will be processed.

That’s it! PhonePe Payment Gateway is successfully integrated with your Shopify store, and you’re now ready to start accepting payments from your customers.
