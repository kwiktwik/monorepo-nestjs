Customer Identifiers can be closed in two ways, automatically and manually.

## Close Customer Identifiers Automatically

To close Customer Identifiers automatically, specify a **Close By** date and time during [Customer Identifier creation](/razorpay-docs-md/smart-collect/create.md).

![Enable/Disable Auto close by while creating Virtual Account.](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/smart-collect/build/browser/assets/images/smart-collect-auto-close-by.jpg)

## Close Customer Identifiers Manually

To close Customer Identifiers manually:

1. Log in to the Dashboard and click **Smart Collect**.
2. Select the Customer Identifier id you wish to close from the Customer Identifier list.

   ![Customer Identifier List](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/smart-collect/build/browser/assets/images/ci-list-sm.jpg)
3. In the right pane that appears, click **Close Customer Identifier**.

   ![Smart Collect Close Button](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/smart-collect/build/browser/assets/images/sm-ci-close-button.jpg)
4. A dialog box appears to confirm the closing. Click **Yes**.
