## Late Authorisation

Late authorisation is a situation that arises when a payment is interrupted by external factors such as network issues or technical errors at the customer's or bank's end. In such cases, funds may or may not get debited from the customer's bank account, and Razorpay does not receive a payment status from the bank. Know more about [Late Authorization](/razorpay-docs-md/payments/late-authorisation.md).
