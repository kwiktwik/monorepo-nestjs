Success rate is the **ratio of successful transactions to the total number of transactions.**
**Success Rate** (SR)=Total number of successful (authorised) transactions / Total number of attempted transactions over a given period of time.
**Example**

If you had 100 transactions in a week and 93 were successful, your success rate would be 93% for that week.

View the success rate of all your transactions and perform the various actions:

- [All Payment Methods](/razorpay-docs-md/payments/success-rate-analytics.md#all-payment-methods)
- [UPI](/razorpay-docs-md/payments/success-rate-analytics.md#upi)
- [Cards](/razorpay-docs-md/payments/success-rate-analytics.md#cards)

## Access Success Rate Analytics (SRA) Dashboard

To access the Success Rate Analytics (SRA) Dashboard:

1. Log in to your Dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Transactions** → **Success Rate**.

   ![navigate to core success rate on Razorpay Dashboard](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/payments/build/browser/assets/images/core-sr-dashboard-navigate.jpg)

Using the Analytics Dashboard, you can:

- Perform a detailed analysis of your recent transactions.
- View the details of each payment method by clicking on the respective method options such as Cards, UPI, Netbanking and Emandate.

  ![analytics view of core success rate on Razorpay Dashboard](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/payments/build/browser/assets/images/core-sr-dashboard-overview.jpg)
- View the payment volume distribution with the help of a pie diagram and understand the top reasons behind the payment failures. Know more about [failure reasons](/razorpay-docs-md/payments/success-rate-analytics.md#payment-failure-reasons)  .

  ![payment volume distribution on Razorpay Dashboard](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/payments/build/browser/assets/images/core-sr-dashboard-overview-two.jpg)
- Correlate downtime with your success rate trends for specific methods.

## Date Range

Select the date and time for which you want to perform a success rate analysis. You can use a predefined range or opt for a custom range.

![core success rate graph](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/payments/build/browser/assets/images/core-sr-date-range.jpg)

**Handy Tips**

- The default date range is 6 hours.
- You can select the date range from upto the last 90 days.
- You can also select a specific time range as per your requirement.

## All Payment Methods

**All Payment Methods** provide a high-level view of your transactions and recent performance at the method level (Cards, UPI, Netbanking and Emandate).

With the **All Payment Methods** option, you can:

- View the success rate at the **payment method** level (UPI, Cards, Netbanking and Emandate).

  ![view core success rate for all methods on Razorpay Dashboard](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/payments/build/browser/assets/images/core-sr-dashboard-all-methods.jpg)
- The line graph shows the success rate distribution of all payment methods on an hourly, daily, weekly and monthly basis as per your date range selection.

  ![view core sr graph for all methods](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/payments/build/browser/assets/images/core-sr-all-methods-graphical.jpg)
- View the payment volume distribution at the method level with the pie chart.

  ![core sr pie diagram all methods](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/payments/build/browser/assets/images/core-sr-pie-diagram.jpg)
- View the top failure reasons along with the breakdown. Know more about [failure reasons](/razorpay-docs-md/payments/success-rate-analytics.md#payment-failure-reasons)  .
