You must provide your live website or mobile app details during account activation.

- Without these details, you will not be able to access live mode API keys and hence **cannot accept payments from your website or app**.
- You can still accept payments from customers using **Invoices** and **Payment Links** generated via the Dashboard.

## Add and Edit Website/App/Additional Websites

Add Website/Mobile App

Update Website/Mobile App

Additional Website/Mobile App

To add your website or mobile app details:

1. Log in to the Dashboard.
2. Click **Account & Settings** in the left navigation.
3. Click **Business website detail** under **Website and app settings** section.

   ![add website/app](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/dashboard/account-settings/build/browser/assets/images/acc-settings-dashboard-biz-website-details.jpg)
4. Click **Add website/app details**.

   ![add website/app](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/dashboard/account-settings/build/browser/assets/images/biz-website-details.jpg)
5. Click **Proceed to update website/app**.

   ![add website/app](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/dashboard/account-settings/build/browser/assets/images/proceed-update-biz-website-details.jpg)
6. Select where you want to collect payments: **Website or App**

   ![add website/app](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/dashboard/account-settings/build/browser/assets/images/website-app-add-dashboard.jpg)
7. Add test account **username and password**.

   **Handy Tips**

   Ensure you have the necessary pages available on your website or app:

   1. Terms and Conditions
   2. Privacy Policy
   3. Shipping Policy
   4. Contact Us
   5. Cancellation and Refunds

   ![add website/app](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/dashboard/account-settings/build/browser/assets/images/dashboard-add-business-website-details-test-acc-details.jpg)
8. Click **Submit** website for review.

- If you add or update your **primary website**, instant checks are run to validate your website, policy pages and business industry. In case the website requirements are not met, you can re-check and enter the valid URLs as prompted or create policy pages with Razorpay.

![Update website checks](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/dashboard/account-settings/build/browser/assets/images/website-dashboard-checks.jpg)

- If you add or update **additional websites**, our team will review manually and notify you.
