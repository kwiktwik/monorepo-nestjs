You can use the Razorpay Payments Mobile app to issue partial or full refunds to your customers.

## How to Issue a Refund

Watch this video to see how to issue a refund. [

Business Verification for a Registered Business

](/razorpay-docs-md/mobile-app/track-manage/build/browser/assets/images/issue-refund.md)

Follow these steps to issue a refund:

1. Log in with your Dashboard credentials. You can also use the **Login With Google** option. If you have enabled the 2FA feature on your account, complete the OTP process.

   ![Payments Mobile App Login](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/mobile-app/track-manage/build/browser/assets/images/payments-mobile-app-login.jpg)
2. Navigate to **Transactions** → **Payments**. Tap on the payment for which you have to issue a refund.
3. Tap **Issue Refund**.

   ![Issue Refunds using Razorpay Mobile App](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/mobile-app/track-manage/build/browser/assets/images/payments-mobile-app-refund.jpg)
4. Provide the following details:

   - Enter the refund amount. You can choose to make a full or partial refund.
   - Select **Refund Instantly** if you want the refund to reach the customer within a day's time.

   **Handy Tips**

   - Instant refund carries additional fees.
   - This feature is available only on Netbanking, UPI and select debit and credit cards. [Check the complete list of payment methods.](/razorpay-docs-md/refunds/instant.md#payment-methods)

   - You can add a comment. This is an optional field.
5. Tap **Issue Full/Partial Refund**.
6. Tap **Yes, Issue Refund** to confirm. You get a confirmation popup as shown:

   ![successful refund](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/mobile-app/track-manage/build/browser/assets/images/payments-mobile-app-success-refund.jpg)
7. To view the details, tap on the payment for which you initiated a refund.
