Items are products or services that you can add to [Invoices](/razorpay-docs-md/invoices.md) and charge customers for. You can create an item on your Razorpay [Dashboard](/razorpay-docs-md/dashboard.md) from **Invoice** → **Items** or using [Items API](/razorpay-docs-md/api/payments/invoices/create-item.md). When an item is created, it appears on the list of items in the Dashboard.
