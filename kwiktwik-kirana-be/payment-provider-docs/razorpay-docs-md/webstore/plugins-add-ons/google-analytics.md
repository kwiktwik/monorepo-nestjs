You can add your Google Tracking ID to your Webstore to track payments and conversions from your Google advertisements. Know more about [Tracking ID](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/1008080?hl=en) and how to create one using Google Analytics account.

**Available Tracking Metrics**

## Prerequisites [Create a Google Tracking ID](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/1008080?hl=en).

## Add Google Tracking ID to Your Webstore

To add Google Tracking ID to your Webstore:

1. Navigate to your Webstore in the edit mode.
2. Click **Page Settings**.
3. Go to **Plugins and Add-ons** and click **Configure**.

   ![Configure Plugins and Add Ons](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/webstore/plugins-add-ons/build/browser/assets/images/pp-plugins-addons-configure.jpg)
4. Add the **Tracking ID** to track the page views.

   ![Add Google Tracking ID and FB Pixel](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/webstore/plugins-add-ons/build/browser/assets/images/pp-plugins-addons-GA.jpg)
5. Click **Save**.
