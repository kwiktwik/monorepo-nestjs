You can configure the shipping rates and create shipping zones on your Shopify Dashboard.

Follow the steps given below:

1. Log in to the [Shopify Store](https://accounts.shopify.com/store-login)

   and navigate to **Settings** → **Shipping and delivery**.

   ![Shopify shipping](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/cod-magic-checkout/shopify/build/browser/assets/images/magic-checkout-shopify-shipping.jpg)
2. Create a profile under general or custom shipping rates and add the required details.

   - **General Shipping Rates**: Create a profile for all products. With this, you charge a standard shipping rate on certain products.
   - **Custom Shipping Rates**: Create an individual shipping rate for a certain product.

   **Watch Out!**

   You cannot list the same products under general and custom shipping rates. You can list a product under only one shipping rate.

   ![Profile for general or custom shipping rates](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/cod-magic-checkout/shopify/build/browser/assets/images/magic-checkout-shopify-profile.jpg)
3. After creating a profile, click **Manage** to navigate to the shipping settings under that profile.

   ![Edit shipping settings](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/cod-magic-checkout/shopify/build/browser/assets/images/magic-checkout-shopify-manage.jpg)
4. Click **Create shipping zone** and add zones of your choice. Click **Done**.

   ![Shipping zones](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/cod-magic-checkout/shopify/build/browser/assets/images/magic-checkout-shopify-shipping-zone.jpg)
5. Click **Add rate** to add the shipping rates. YYou can create multiple shipping rates or options to display at checkout.

   ![Add shipping rates](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/cod-magic-checkout/shopify/build/browser/assets/images/magic-checkout-shopify-add-rate.jpg)
6. Select **Set up your own rates**. Follow the steps given below:

   1. Enter your **Rate name**, for example, Standard Shipping Charges. Customers will view this at Checkout.
   2. Enter the **Price**.
   3. Add conditions if required. You can add conditions based on **item weight** or **order price**. Enter the required details.
   4. Click **Done**.

      ![Set up your own rates](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/cod-magic-checkout/shopify/build/browser/assets/images/magic-checkout-shopify-own-rate.jpg)

**Handy Tips**

Ensure all shipping methods from Shopify are listed on the Dashboard. In **COD Settings**, click **Sync Again** to update and sync the latest shipping methods from Shopify.

![Configure COD for shopify shipping methods](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/cod-magic-checkout/shopify/build/browser/assets/images/cod-magic-config-cod.jpg)
