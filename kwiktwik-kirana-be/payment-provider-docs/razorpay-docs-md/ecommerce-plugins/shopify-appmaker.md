Appmaker allows you to create a custom mobile app for your Shopify store, providing a seamless shopping experience for your customers. Integrate with the Razorpay Shopify - Appmaker extension to offer a smooth payment experience to your customers on the app.

Follow the steps given below to integrate the Razorpay Payment Gateway with Shopify - Appmaker on your Shopify store.

#### Standard Checkout

Integrate Razorpay Appmaker Extension With Shopify for Standard Checkout.

#### Magic Checkout

Integrate Razorpay Appmaker Extension With Shopify for Magic Checkout.

## Prerequisites

Before you proceed, make sure you have the following:

- Sign up for a [Razorpay account](https://dashboard.razorpay.com/signup)  .
- Sign up for a [Shopify account](https://www.shopify.in)  .
- Integrate [Appmaker](https://apps.shopify.com/appmaker)

  with Shopify.
- [Integrate Razorpay with Shopify](/razorpay-docs-md/payment-gateway/ecommerce-plugins/shopify.md)

  .

## Integration Steps

Follow the steps given below:

1. Integrate Razorpay Appmaker Extension With Shopify for Standard Checkout

Follow these steps to proceed with the integration:

1. After adding AppMaker to your Shopify store, navigate to the AppMaker window. Click **Extensions**.

   ![Appmaker window extension](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/ecommerce-plugins/build/browser/assets/images/appmaker-extension.jpg)
2. On the Installed Extensions page, click **Explore Extensions**.

   ![Explore extension](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/ecommerce-plugins/build/browser/assets/images/explore-extension.jpg)
3. Search for Razorpay in the search bar on the All Extensions page. Select **Razorpay-Shopify-Appmaker** and click **Next**.

   ![All extensions razorpay shopify appmaker](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/ecommerce-plugins/build/browser/assets/images/razorpay-shopify-appmaker.jpg)
4. Click **Install & Activate**.

   ![Install and activate razorpay shopify appmaker](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/ecommerce-plugins/build/browser/assets/images/install-activate-rsa.jpg)

Appmaker extension is successfully installed and activated.

2. Integrate Razorpay Appmaker Extension With Shopify for Magic Checkout

Follow this step to proceed with the integration:

You must do the [standard checkout integration](/razorpay-docs-md/ecommerce-plugins/shopify-appmaker.md#1-integrate-razorpay-appmaker-extension-with-shopify-for) before proceeding with the Magic checkout integration. After you install the Razorpay Shopify Appmaker extension, navigate to **Settings** and enter the **Config Key**. Click **Save Changes**.

![Razorpay shopify appmaker config key](https://razorpay.com/docs/payments/ecommerce-plugins/build/browser/assets/images/rsa-config-key.jpg)

Once you save the changes, the Appmaker extension will be available with Magic Checkout on the Native SDKs.
