Attract customers and improve sales by providing discounts or cashback on your website or apps. Using Razorpay Offers, you can completely control the discounts offered to your customers. You can restrict the payment methods on which the Offers are applied and limit their usage to a defined period.

**Watch Out!**

We do not support offers on international currency and the CFB (Customer Fee Bearer) model.

Advantages

- Create and run attractive offers on your website, thereby increasing sales.
- Provide interest-free EMI options with No Cost EMI Offers.
- With the help of Low Cost EMI Offers, provide upfront discounts by splitting the interest cost with your customers.
- Grow conversions of price-sensitive customers.
- Enjoy customer loyalty by providing offers to returning customers.
- Incremental offers lead to higher average order value, thereby boosting sales.

## Customer Experience

Customers have the opportunity to see the offers that you configure on checkout. They can assess and decide which offer best suits their needs and provides the most advantages or benefits. Additionally, Razorpay will automatically apply the most suitable offer to the chosen payment method, ensuring a seamless and optimised experience for the customer.
